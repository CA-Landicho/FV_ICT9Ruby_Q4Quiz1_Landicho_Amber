Take a Glimpse into the Beautiful Philippines!


Go to the bottom

The Philippines is composed of 7641 diverse islands, full of warm, welcoming, and hospitable natives. When you visit the Philippines you will surely be amazed at how diverse yet united our people are. So to get started on your journey, here are a few of the many marvels of our nation!




Oslob Whale Shark Interaction

Swim with the majestic whale sharks, or opt for whale shark watching in Oslob, Cebu. Witness and be amazed at the diverse marine life and their vivid colors!

Boracay

Frolick in the white sand of Boracay's paradise-like beaches and swim in its crystal clear waters while paddleboarding or snorkeling along its shores!



Puerto Princesa Underground River

Explore the stalactites and stalagmites in the caves of the Puerto Princesa Underground River while kayaking across the crystal rivers.

Baguio City

Come cool off and take a road trip to Baguio City, the Summer Capital of the Philippines where you can hand pick strawberries!



Chocolate Hills

Marvel at the ever changing Chocolate Hills of Bohol. Take a climb and snap some cool pictures at its summit!

Banaue Rice Terraces

Prepare yourself for the climb and the immersive history behind the Banaue Rice Terraces!
